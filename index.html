Our story starts in the grove.

Akcal is a family-run olive oil producer focused on fresh harvest oils and transparent, small-batch production. We work with early morning picks, gentle washing, and cold pressing to preserve the aroma and polyphenols that define premium extra virgin olive oil.

Every bottle is traceable to the lot and harvest date. We serve both retail and hospitality partners who want a consistent, clean profile for table, kitchen, and finishing.

What we protect

  • Single-estate olives
  • Cold chain from press to storage
  • No blending with aged oils
  • Lab-tested with every harvest
